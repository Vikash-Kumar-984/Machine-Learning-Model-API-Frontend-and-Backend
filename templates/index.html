Welcome to Birth Weight Predictor

Enter the medical information to predict the birth weight of a newborn.

Description

Gestation: Number of weeks the baby was in the womb. Normally between 37 to 42 weeks (provide input in days).

Parity: Number of times the mother has previously given birth(0 = first pregnancy).

Age: Age of the mother in years.

Height: Height of the mother in inches .

Weight: Pre-pregnancy weight of the mother in pounds.

Smoke: Mother smoking status (0 = Non-smoker, 1 = Smoker).

BWT: Birthweight in ounces (Result) .
